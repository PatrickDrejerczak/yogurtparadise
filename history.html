Welcome to Yogurt Paradise!

Our lovely Ms. Tam is not only a pretty lady, but also an experienced yogurt chef with over 100.000 yogurt jars of experience. Tam Tam´s Yogurt Paradies is an family run business that operates since 2018 and aims to achieve yogurt perfection. The masterly designed flavors of Yogurt Paradise yogurts are ment to please everybody´s taste buds.

Prior to her carrer at Tam Tam´s Yogurt Paradies, Ms. Tam learned from the best yogurt makers in the world, travelling to Germany and Switzerland to the real yogurt artisans. Her passion for yogurt developed through the years and she developed her own recipe with the help of international yogurt experts. The flavorful milk, paired with mystical ingredients and Ms. Tams love, we guarantee that Tam Tam´s Yogurt Paradise provides a true taste of Paradise.

Throughout the years, Ms. Tam also learned to make her own cheese and her passion for that grew. Therefore, the all-time classic in our Paradise is the Cheese Yogurt, that is made with experience in yogurt and cheese making and combines all of the masterful skills our chef has. But also our other flavors are carefully thought through and tuned into perfection. Please, enjoy our yogurts and become a part of our family. Come and taste true Paradise.
